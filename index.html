About Me

Hi there! My name is Slava Pashaliuk and I am a Software Engineer based in Los Angeles, CA.


Since early childhood I was passionate about computers and the fact how good are they at following instructions. It was fascinating to me to see how machines are able to do so much by simply switching between 0's and 1's. And still is.


My Computer Science journey is a bit irregular one, but this only means that I have more fun nerdy stories to tell. Initially I started at College as most people that are in the field right now, but then I had to skip a year of school. While on the unplanned break I went through the Software Engineering bootcamp, found the internship next summer at SevOne Inc. and loved the place so much that I decided to stay there as a part-time Software Engineer, while finishing my last year of studying towards BS in Computer Science.
